Journey from ice-cream shop to own manufacturing

Rock n Roll ice-cream company is owned and founded by Mr. Bhagwan Dass Kataria which is situated in Lane Number 11, Gandhi Colony, Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh (251001). Since, 2000 we are in the market because we are serving quality ice-cream to our customers. After lot of hardwork and dedication of Mr. Bhagwan Dass Kataria, Mr. Bharat Kataria (CEO), and Mr. Lokesh Kataria (Manager) company gained his popularity in Uttar-Pradesh. Now, recently we inaugrated our new parlour "THE ICE-GARDEN". Our main priority is customer's satisfaction and product quality. So explore our website to study about our products.
